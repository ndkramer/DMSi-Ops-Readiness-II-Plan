Key Structural Insights

Three Parallel Tracks

Operational (process redesign), Environmental (people and alignment), and Technological (tooling) run as interlocking but distinct tracks. Environmental setup (E1-E3) bridges both WSB and WSC, creating the cross-functional alignment both workstreams depend on.

Iterative by Design

O2, O4, E3, and T6 are explicitly iterative. The operating model and tooling both expect multiple socialization and refinement cycles before institutionalization. This is built into the plan, not a sign of rework.

Technology Follows Process

T1 (capability audit) cannot produce meaningful requirements until O1 (data/segmentation) and E1 (role definitions) establish what the operating model needs. The RFP process (T2) depends on both operational and environmental clarity.
